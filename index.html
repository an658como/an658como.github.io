About Me

I am a passionate learner dedicated to both my career and professional development as well as my personal interests. Whether it's mastering new challenges or refining existing abilities, I approach learning with enthusiasm, determination, and a strong sense of perseverance. In addition to my dedication to learning, I'm also a professional musician. I specialize in playing the Tombak, a traditional Persian hand drum, and have performed in various concerts and gigs. Alongside my Tombak journey, I'm also learning to play the guitar and exploring my voice as a singer. Beyond music, I'm an active and sporty individual. I enjoy weightlifting and outdoor activities like hiking, and I'm also involved in semi-professional volleyball and rock climbing. Fitness is a key part of my lifestyle, keeping both my mind and body in top shape.

In my spare time, I indulge in my love for photography. Capturing moments through the lens allows me to express my creativity in another medium. Feel free to check out my photo portfolio.
